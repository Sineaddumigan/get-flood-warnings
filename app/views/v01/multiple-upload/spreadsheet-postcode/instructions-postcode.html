How to upload a spreadsheet with addresses and postcodes

  • Download and fill in the address and postcodes templates (xls)
  • include the full address, with postcodes, for each location for example:House of Commons, London, SW1A 0AA
  • we recommend using the criticality field to prioritise your locations so if there is a flood you can filter by criticality and act on the highly critical locations to your business first
  • if you are not using our template, you can match your fields to ours after you upload your file. We will ask for:
    • location name
    • full address
    • postcode
    • location reference (optional)
    • location type (optional)
    • location team (optional)
    • action to be taken (optional)
    • criticality (optional)
    • notes (optional)
  • you can upload your spreadsheet on the next page
